About Tammy Lynn Merson, PMHNP

Tammy Lynn Merson is a psychiatric nurse practitioner serving clients in and around Goldsboro, Maryland. She works with individuals seeking support for mood, anxiety, and related mental health concerns, with care plans that combine clinical expertise and practical everyday strategies.

Her practice emphasizes clear communication, informed decision-making, and treatment that respects each person's lived experience and goals.

Approach & philosophy

Care at Shore Psychiatric Services is collaborative and paced to the individual. Visits are designed to create a clear understanding of what you're experiencing, then build a treatment path that may include medication management, follow-up monitoring, and practical adjustments over time.

The goal is steady progress, better function, and long-term support rather than one-size-fits-all care.
