About Tammy Lynn Merson, PMHNP

Tammy Lynn Merson is a psychiatric mental health nurse practitioner serving adults in Maryland. Her work includes psychiatric evaluation, diagnosis, and medication management with a focus on clear communication and practical treatment plans.

Her background includes supporting people navigating anxiety, depressive symptoms, mood concerns, and attention-related challenges. Care is collaborative and paced to each person, with room for thoughtful adjustment over time.

Appointments are offered with sensitivity to each client's daily responsibilities and goals, including telehealth options across Maryland.

Approach to care

Collaborative planning

Visits are built around shared decision-making, so treatment reflects your history, values, and comfort level.

Clinical clarity

Evaluation and follow-up focus on specific symptoms, response to treatment, and clear understanding of options.

Steady follow-through

Ongoing appointments help refine care so progress is realistic, measurable, and sustainable.
