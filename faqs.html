Frequently Asked Questions

Who do you see?

Shore Psychiatric Services works with Maryland clients who need psychiatric assessment and medication-focused treatment.

How does telehealth work?

Telehealth appointments are available when you are physically located in Maryland. A reliable internet connection and private location are recommended for each visit.

How do I schedule and prepare for my first appointment?

Start by contacting the office by phone or through the website form. For your first visit, plan to discuss your symptoms, health history, and treatment goals in detail.

Do you accept insurance?

Coverage and billing details can vary. Please contact the office directly for the most current information about fees, payment, and insurance options.

What if I am in crisis?

This practice is not an emergency service. If you are in immediate danger or may harm yourself or others, call or text 988 right away, or call 911.
