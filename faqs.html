Frequently asked questions

Who can be seen at Shore Psychiatric Services?

Appointments are available for Maryland clients seeking psychiatric evaluation and medication-focused treatment.

How does telehealth work?

Telehealth visits are available while you are in Maryland. You will need a private setting and stable internet connection.

What happens during an initial appointment?

The first appointment usually covers mental health history, current symptoms, and treatment options tailored to your needs.

Do you provide emergency psychiatric care?

No. This practice does not provide emergency services. If you are in immediate danger or crisis, call or text 988, or call 911.

Can I send private medical details through the website form?

Please do not include sensitive medical information in web or email messages. Use the form for general contact only.
