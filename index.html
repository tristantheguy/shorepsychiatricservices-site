Frequently asked questions

Who do you work with?

Care is available for Maryland clients seeking psychiatric evaluation and medication support.

Do you offer virtual appointments?

Yes. Telehealth visits are available for clients located in Maryland at the time of their appointment.

What should I expect for a first visit?

Your first session typically includes a full history review, symptom discussion, and initial treatment planning.

What if I have an urgent safety concern?

This practice is not an emergency service. In immediate crisis, call or text 988, or call 911.
