Services

Psychiatric treatment plans built for clarity, consistency, and meaningful improvement.

Comprehensive Psychiatric Evaluations

Initial assessments review symptoms, history, prior treatment, and current concerns to establish a thoughtful diagnosis and plan.

Medication Management

Medication recommendations are individualized and monitored closely, with follow-up adjustments based on response and tolerability.

Telehealth Psychiatric Care

Virtual visits are available for Maryland residents, helping reduce travel burden while maintaining continuity of care.

What to expect at your first visit

  • Review of current symptoms and goals
  • Mental health and medical history discussion
  • Medication history and treatment planning
  • Time for your questions and next-step planning

What to expect at follow-ups

  • Progress check and symptom tracking
  • Medication effectiveness and side effect review
  • Plan refinement and refill coordination
  • Scheduling based on your clinical needs
